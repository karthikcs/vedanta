| **Name of Prana** | **Function described in text** | **Physical Role**                                                                                          |
| ----------------- | ------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Prāṇa**         | Forward/Outward                | Respiration; the breath associated with the heart and nostrils.                                            |
| **Apāna**         | Leading away                   | Excretion; it moves downward to remove waste (_mūtra-purīṣa_ - urine/feces).                               |
| **Samāna**        | Equalizing                     | Digestion; located in the navel, it assimilates food and drink.                                            |
| **Udāna**         | Moving upward                  | Ascending; it is the "wind" that rises to the throat/head and carries the soul out at death.               |
| **Vyāna**         | Pervading                      | Circulation; it is the pervasive energy that distributes nutrients and is responsible for systemic health. |
